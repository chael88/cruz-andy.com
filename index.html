About Me

Who am I?

I am an experienced Wordpress developer with a passion for delivering superior client results. I grew up in NJ with an earned Engineering Science degree. What started as a fun hobby creating websites for friends has turned into a full time gig for me.

What's your process?

An initial meeting via phone or Skype with clients is scheduled to go over in-depth about the side, design aesthetics, content & budget. Milestones and delivery dates are scheduled, as well as an estimate. Provided via PDF forms. Nearly all of my projects come in on time and under budget. I work hard to keep it that way! I specialize in front end development (HTML, CSS, jQuery) for custom work but for ecommerce, subscription, & blogs, I go to Wordpress.

Best way to get in touch?

I love email. By using the contact form, you can expect an email reply within an hour during Central business hours, and usually within more like 20 minutes. I'm also open and flexible about making appointments for real-time conversation. My favorite attachments from clients are PDFs and screenshots (jpg or png). My least favorites are Microsoft Word® docx or PowerPoint® pptx.
